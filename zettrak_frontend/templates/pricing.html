Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ZetTrak pricing.

🤔 What counts as an employee?

Any active employee record added in the system counts. Deactivated or deleted employees do not count towards your limit.

💳 Do I need a credit card for the free plan?

No. The free plan is completely free forever — no credit card, no trial period, no surprises.

📈 What happens if I exceed 20 employees?

You will be prompted to upgrade to the Pro plan. Your existing data is safe and you can contact us to discuss pricing for your team size.

🔄 Can I switch plans anytime?

Yes. You can upgrade from Free to Pro at any time. All your data migrates seamlessly — no downtime required.

🏢 Is there a discount for NGOs or educational institutions?

Yes. We offer special pricing for non-profit organisations and educational institutions. Contact us to learn more.

📞 What support is included in the Free plan?

Free plan users have access to our documentation and community support. Pro plan users get priority email and phone support with an SLA.
